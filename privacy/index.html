Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority. All file processing happens locally in your browser — we never upload, access, or store your files.

Last updated: July 1, 2026

1. No Data Collection on File Processing

All image conversion and PDF operations performed on FastConvert happen entirely within your web browser using client-side JavaScript and the HTML5 Canvas API. Your files are never uploaded to any server. We do not collect, store, transmit, or have access to any files you process using our tools. Once you close or refresh your browser tab, all file data is permanently discarded from memory.

This applies to all FastConvert tools, including image conversion (PNG, JPG, WebP, HEIC), image compression, image resizing, image cropping, PDF merging, PDF splitting, PDF compression, PDF rotation, and image-to-PDF conversion.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect personal identification information (name, email, address) unless voluntarily submitted via our Contact Us form.
  • We do not collect, store, or transmit your uploaded images or converted files.
  • We do not use server-side processing for file conversions.
  • We do not use cookies for tracking your file processing activities.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share any user data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Local Storage for User Preferences

We use your browser's local storage strictly to remember your user interface preferences, such as your choice of light or dark theme. This data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers. We do not use local storage or first-party cookies to track your behavior across the website.

4. Google AdSense & Cookies

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google AdSense and its partners may use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites on the Internet.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. Alternatively, you can opt out of third-party vendor cookies for personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info.

For more details about how Google uses data from sites that use their services, please visit Google's Partner Sites Policy.

5. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors engage with our site — such as which pages are most popular, how long visitors stay, and what devices they use. Google Analytics may use cookies to track visitor interactions. This information helps us improve our tools and user experience.

Google Analytics collects anonymized, aggregate data. We do not link this data to any personally identifiable information. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

6. Third-Party Services

Apart from Google AdSense and Google Analytics, we may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Fonts: We load fonts from Google's servers to ensure consistent typography. Google may log standard web request data (IP address, browser type). See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • CDN Services: We use Content Delivery Networks (jsDelivr, unpkg) to load open-source JavaScript libraries (heic2any, pdf-lib). These CDNs may log standard access data.

7. Log Data

Our hosting provider may collect standard server log information, including IP addresses, browser type, referring/exit pages, and timestamps. This data is used solely for website maintenance and security and is not linked to any personally identifiable information.

8. Contact Form Data

If you choose to reach out via our Contact Us page or email us directly, any information you provide (name, email, message) will be used solely to respond to your inquiry. We do not sell, share, or use this information for marketing purposes.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately so we can take appropriate action.

10. Data Security

Since FastConvert processes all files locally in your browser, the security of your files is inherently protected — they never traverse the internet. For website communications, we use HTTPS encryption to protect data transmitted between your browser and our servers (such as page content and analytics data).

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Your continued use of the website after any changes constitutes acceptance of the new Privacy Policy. We recommend checking this page periodically for updates.

12. Your Consent

By using our website, you consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.

13. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us via the Contact Us page or email us directly at support.paniapp@gmail.com.
