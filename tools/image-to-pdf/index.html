Why Convert Images to PDF?

Discover the benefits of compiling your photos into a single PDF file.

Creating Portfolios and Presentations

If you're a designer, photographer, or student, presenting your work as a collection of loose image files can look unprofessional and be difficult for the recipient to view. Converting your images into a single PDF creates a clean, organized portfolio that anyone can open on any device. The pages will stay in the exact order you arranged them, ensuring your work is presented exactly as intended.

Sharing Scanned Documents

When you take photos of receipts, ID cards, or handwritten notes with your phone, they are saved as JPG or HEIC files. While fine for personal use, businesses and government portals usually require these documents to be submitted in PDF format. Our tool lets you quickly compile these photos into a standard A4 or Letter-sized PDF that looks like a properly scanned document.

Page Size Options Explained

Our tool gives you three options for the output PDF:
• A4: The standard paper size used globally. Best for documents you plan to print.
• Letter: The standard paper size used in North America.
• Fit to Image: The PDF page will match the exact dimensions of your image. This is perfect for digital portfolios where you don't want any white borders around your artwork.
