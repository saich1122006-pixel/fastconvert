About Us

FastConvert is built by a small, passionate team of utility-software engineers who believe powerful tools should be free, fast, and private. We started this project with a simple mission: give everyone access to professional-grade image conversion without the bloatware, paywalls, or privacy invasions.

Our Philosophy

Every file you convert stays on your device — period. We don't run servers that touch your data, we don't store anything, and we never will. Our tool runs entirely in your browser using modern web standards like the HTML5 Canvas API. It's fast, it's secure, and it respects your privacy by design.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2026

FastConvert ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you visit and use our website.

1. No Data Collection on File Processing

All image conversion performed on FastConvert happens entirely within your web browser using client-side JavaScript and the HTML5 Canvas API. Your files are never uploaded to any server. We do not collect, store, transmit, or have access to any files you process using our tool. Once you close or refresh your browser tab, all file data is permanently discarded from memory.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect personal identification information (name, email, address) unless voluntarily submitted via our Contact Us form.
  • We do not collect, store, or transmit your uploaded images or converted files.
